About

This project introduces a comprehensive tool for backtesting trading strategies based on Simple Moving Averages (SMA) and Exponential Moving Averages (EMA). The tool allows users to simulate trading strategies by applying SMA and EMA indicators to historical price data. By executing these simulations, the tool provides detailed performance metrics, including potential profit and loss calculations, to help users assess the effectiveness of their strategies.

Technical Information

Implemented in Python, the tool utilizes Pandas for robust data manipulation and time series analysis, and NumPy for efficient numerical computations. It generates comprehensive reports and visualizations with Matplotlib, showcasing the simulated trading outcomes, transaction costs, and overall financial performance. This enables users to refine their strategies based on historical data and make informed decisions for future trading.
