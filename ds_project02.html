Technical Information

In technical analysis, momentum refers to the rate of acceleration of a security's price, helping traders identify the strength and direction of a trend. The momentum of a stock price can be defined by the formula:

M = Pn - Pn-1

where P is the price at each period and Pn-1 is the price of the previous day.

By measuring how quickly the price changes, momentum indicators, such as the Relative Strength Index (RSI) or Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD), provide insights into whether a trend is gaining or losing momentum.

The project includes the implementation of buy and sell indicators based on these moving averages, enabling users to make informed investment decisions.

This project was built in Python programming language using the Pandas library, among others.
