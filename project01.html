About

Using computer vision techniques, this project detects free and occupied parking spaces with just a few cameras.

The use of cameras for this application generates great savings in the implementation and maintenance of parking lots when compared to conventional methods such as infrared sensors.

Technical Information

This project was composed of two approaches .

In the first approach , image processing techniques such as RGB to HSV color space conversion and Histogram of Oriented Gradients were used together with supervised machine learning algorithms such as SVM and Random Forest to determine an algorithm that presented robustness for the detection of vacancies in open environments under natural lighting. The training and validation step were done using techniques K-Fold cross validation and Confusion Matrix respectively .

The second approach proposes a lighter algorithm where only image processing techniques were used to determine available vacancies.
In this approach were used filtering operations such as Grayscale conversion, Blur , Adaptative Threshold , Median and Dilate .
