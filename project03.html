About

Using computer vision techniques, this project detects if an individual has loss of focus while driving a vehicle caused by sleep or tiredness.

This project makes it possible to alert vehicle drivers at times when loss of attention is detected while driving, helping to prevent accidents and ensure safe driving.

Technical Information

This project uses Google's Media Pipe artificial intelligence to detect faces and face landmarks .

From the detected fiducial points, the openCV library is used to calculate the distance between the fiducial points of each eye. In scenarios where the distance of the fiducial points of the eyes are below a pre-defined value, it is considered that the individual is about to close his eyes, characterizing a loss of focus.

In parallel, the distance from the fiducial points of the mouth is also measured, in order to identify yawning scenarios, which are also considered as a sign that the vehicle is being driven by an individual who shows signs of fatigue.
